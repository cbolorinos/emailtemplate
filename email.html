REQUEST A SATURNO GDPR AUDIT OF YOUR DIGITAL MARKETING TOOLS

The new regulations go into effect on 5/25

While personalized consultation is available for each Saturno client, common areas to review include:

SUBMISSIONS FORMS

Guidance and code updates on consent options, form security, and appropriate fields for forms used on websites for contact, subscription or registration purposes.

PROFILE MANAGEMENT

User profile management tools allowing end users to exercise their GDPR rights, including requesting their full profile information, making updates to their profile, and deleting their profile.

PROOF OF CONSENT

Electronic storage of consent whenever users opt-in on a relevant submission form on the website. This information is stored in an exportable format that can then be used as demonstrable proof of consent given at a specific date and time.

TRACKING COOKIES

Guidance on website cookies including explanations of cookie functions, identification of cookies that collect personally identifiable information, and implementation of pop-up dialogs that prompt uses to opt-in to cookie tracking in a GDPR compliant manner.

EMAIL MARKETING

For firms using Saturno’s Email Marketing Center, new tools have been built allowing full export or deletion of user profiles and tracking data, consent request email campaigns, and additional CRM integration options to write user GDPR related preferences and actions to the firm’s CRM.
